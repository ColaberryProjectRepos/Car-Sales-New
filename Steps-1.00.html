Problem Statement 1

The alarming rise in car sales is contributing to a pressing environmental concern, as evidenced by the projected statistics. According to the International Energy Agency (IEA), global fuel consumption for road transportation is expected to reach 1.7 billion metric tons of oil equivalent (Mtoe) by 2040, reflecting a significant increase from the 1.3 billion Mtoe consumed in 2019. This surge in fuel consumption is directly linked to the growing number of cars on the road, leading to the depletion of finite fossil fuel reserves and exacerbating environmental degradation caused by extraction and transportation processes.
