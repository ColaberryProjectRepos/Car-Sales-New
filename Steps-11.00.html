Refresh Date Table 11


1. On the Power BI Desktop ribbon, Click on Transform data

2.  Click on the New Source button, select Blank query from the list, and click on Connect

3. Under properties in the Name box, type in 'Refresh Date.'

4.  On the Dax formula bar, type ' = DateTimeZone.LocalNow()'

5.  Click on the Convert to Table button

6.  After the refresh table pops up, rename the column by right-clicking and choosing to rename from the selection

7. Click on the Close & Apply button and save
